Aapno Bank(AB) is an Indian multinational, private sector banking and financial services statutory body headquartered in Banasthali Newai, Jaipur, Rajasthan. AB is the 43rd largest bank in the India and ranked 236th in the Fortune Global 500 list of the India's biggest corporations of 2020.

A nationalised bank, it is the largest in India with a 73% market share by assets and a 25% share of the total loan and deposits market.

AB Bank was incorporated in September 2000. As of September 30, 2020, the Bank had a nationwide distribution network 5,430 branches and 15,292 ATMs in 2,848 cities/towns.
